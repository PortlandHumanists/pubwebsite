Presentation by Robert Schmidt. Slides omitted. According to Immanuel Wallerstein, a world systems analyst, we may be coming to the end of the world as we know it. We may be coming to a world system shift similar to the Fall to the Roman Empire, or to the end of Feudalism and the beginning of the nation-state system. Robert Schmidt has a Ph.D. in Political Science. He has taught in various countries. Before his retirement he was a professor of Theology and Political Science at Concordia University in Portland.
