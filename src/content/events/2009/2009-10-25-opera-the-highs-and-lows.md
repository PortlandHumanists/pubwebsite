Presented by HGP member David Almond. The talk will provide some basic information about opera including terms, history, etc. Since opera is all about the voice, different voice types are illustrated through video clips. Also includes a brief exploration of opera versus musical theater, modern opera and the ultimate operatic heavyweight, Wagner's Ring Cycle. And there won't be a fat lady, so you'll never know when it's over.
