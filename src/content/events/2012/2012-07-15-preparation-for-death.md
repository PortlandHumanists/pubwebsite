This is a presentation by Del Allen about preparing for expected and unexpected end of life, accidents, fire, wills and trusts etc. Over many years, Del has been collecting information from many organizations/sources regarding end of life preparations. He recently started organizing this accumulation so I can get some use out of it and at the same time hopefully get my affairs in order. He presents a list of questions one can ask oneself and/or a professional plus some suggestions and a few facts from various resources.
