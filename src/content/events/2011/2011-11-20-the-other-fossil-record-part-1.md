This is a presentation by Dr. Jon Peters. With the sequencing of the human genome and that of other organisms, we can now compare DNA sequences between species. What has emerged is some of the most compelling evidence for evolution, and DNA observations that contradict most interpretations of Genesis. Topics discussed included DNA evidence for human migration, human chromosome 2 fusion, ERVs, pseudogenes, atavisms, and other related topics.
